Venue Information

Indonesia's most trusted forum for organizing highly acclaimed exhibitions, the Jakarta International Expo (JIExpo) offers a wide range of great services and venue options for its exhibitors. The expo center provides modular spaces which can vary anywhere between 5,000 sq mts to 26,000 sq mts, depending upon the type of event. The Jakarta International Expo has been custom-designed to meet the needs of the exhibitors and are known for their superb catering and land services. One can also easily avail of JIExpo's other facilities which includes ample parking spaces, easy accessibility, and visitors/organizers can also enjoy full benefits of having a 5 star hotel within its vicinity.
